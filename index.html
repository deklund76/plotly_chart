Demographic Info

The above charts display data on the bacteria content sampled from the navels of survey participants. The bar graph shows the top 10 most common bacteria and their frequencies. The frequency of all different bacteria is displayed in the bubble chart with larger bubbles indicating more prevelant bacteria. The gauge chart shows how often the study participant washes their navel, this is expected to affect the biodiversity of microg-organisms there.


Each study participant is identified by ID numbers to keep their personal information confidential. To view different participants data, select their ID from the dropdown menu in the upper left. The purpose of this study ultimately is to find bacteria well suited to producing proteins for synthetic meat. Hopefully, no one finds out where that meat came from XD.
